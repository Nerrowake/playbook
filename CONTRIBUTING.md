# Contributing to the Nerrowake Playbook

The playbook is the shared standards source for Nerrowake repositories. Changes
should make the standards clearer, more practical, or easier to apply.

## Guidelines

- Keep guidance specific and actionable.
- Prefer durable standards over project-specific details.
- Avoid process for its own sake.
- Update related docs and templates together.
- Preserve the Nerrowake engineering values.

## Review Checklist

- The change improves clarity or maintainability.
- Related docs are updated.
- Templates remain usable as copy-and-adapt starting points.
- No private project details or secrets are included.
